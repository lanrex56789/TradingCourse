How my coaching works

We focus on a process you can repeat — not hype, not “get rich quick.”

1) Assess

We review your experience, time availability, and common mistakes. Then we set realistic goals.

2) Build a plan

We define setups, rules, and risk limits. You learn exactly what to do before you ever click buy/sell.

3) Execute + review

We trade (or replay), journal, and review. The goal is consistency and decision quality.

Who this is for

Good fit if you:

  • Want structure and accountability
  • Can commit to journaling and review
  • Are open to improving discipline
  • Prefer clear rules over “signals”

Not a fit if you:

  • Need guaranteed profits
  • Want to gamble with high leverage
  • Don’t want to track results
  • Can’t follow risk limits
